I love hearing from people. Whether you want to talk about something I wrote, recommend a book, share a recipe, or just say hi, I'm here for it.

## Best Way to Reach Me

Email is best. I actually read everything and I try to respond within a few days.

## A Few Things I'm Open To

Guest posts, creative collaborations, and conversations about writing. If you have an idea, send it over. The worst I can say is "not right now," and even then I'll probably say it nicely.

## One Request

If you're reaching out about something I wrote and it resonated with you, please tell me which piece and why. That kind of feedback genuinely makes my whole week.
