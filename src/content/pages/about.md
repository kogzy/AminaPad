Hi, I'm Amina.

## Why This Blog Exists

I started this blog because I kept losing my writing. Napkins, Notes app drafts, random Google Docs with names like "untitled 47." I figured if I put it somewhere real, I might actually finish things. Or at least find them again.

## What I Write About

Honestly, whatever I feel like. That's the nice thing about having your own corner of the internet. Some weeks it's poetry. Some weeks it's a story I've been turning over in my head for months. Sometimes it's just me being excited about a recipe.

I don't have a niche. I don't have a posting schedule. I just have a lot of words and a place to put them.

## The Important Stuff

I believe writing doesn't have to be perfect to be worth sharing. I believe short stories can be two paragraphs long. I believe poems don't have to rhyme. And I believe the best recipes come from messing up and trying again.

If any of that sounds good to you, stick around. I'm glad you're here.
